About Project

This project aims to design a visualization tool which provides a complete and detailed view to help Airbnb hosts to analyze their business from multiple perspectives. It visualizes the hosts’ information from multiple metrics, compare the hosts’ performance with their better-performed neighbours while also providing an overall picture of all hosts’ business performance.

Presented by Xiangyu Fan & Weiyu Wang. Special acknowledgement gives to Prof. Nan Cao.
